Temporary Packages (2 Years)

Live-in domestic workers under Musaned sponsorship for up to 2 years. We handle all documentation, visas, medicals, health insurance and salary processing – so your family gets reliable support without the admin headache.

Fully Managed Sponsorship

We remain the sponsor and take care of every legal and financial detail.

  • MOHRE-compliant contracts in Arabic & English
  • Visa processing, entry permits and renewals handled by Musaned
  • Healthcare, medicals and insurance arranged for the worker
  • Salary processing and documentation managed through us

Your role is to welcome her into your home and treat her well – we take care of the paperwork and ongoing compliance.

Smooth Onboarding & Follow-up

We guide you through each step so you always know what is happening.
